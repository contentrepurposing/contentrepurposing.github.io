Hi, I'm Oluwabunmi.👋🏾

Behind the Studio

I help podcasters, coaches and consultants turn one episode into a full week of content. Show notes, captions, newsletters, pull quotes, video clip timestamps, and so much more. All delivered to your inbox in 24 to 48 hours.

I started this work because I am a podcaster myself. I know what it feels like to pour everything into recording, hit publish, and then watch your episode quietly disappear into the feed. I know how exhausting it is to feel like you are doing all the work and seeing none of the reach.

So I built the support system I wish I had. The one that takes care of everything that comes after the episode. So you can keep recording, keep showing up, and finally watch your work reach the people it was made for.
